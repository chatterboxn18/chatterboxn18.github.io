Drink Water App

I have always wanted an assistant to remind me drink water, and when my favorite artist came back with an album called Like Water, I decided the time had come to create that assistant. I themed the entire application around the two title tracks of the album and the UI design is created around the album's color scheme. The two main functions include a timer that plays rain sounds and a water tracker. When water is consumed and logged, it feeds different plants the user can collect as a visual reward to drinking water. For further motivation, the user can grow certain plants to collect digital photo cards that showcase where the plant was inspired from. In addition, before I made the application, I also created a simple mini game (found in the app) to learn PlayCanvas. Play Here

Created in Unity. Available for Android.

My Interest Game

During the pandemic, my parents and I would play board games together, and when we would chat, they would always ask about what my interests were and what I have been up to, so I came up with this idea that I could make this card game based on all of my interests. The game is based loosely on elements from Monopoly Deal and MahJong so my parents could have an easier time to pick it up as well as learn about my interests. It ended up being a big hit between my friends and I as well and we would spent hours playing at InNOut. The goal of the game is to complete as many sets before all the cards are played out.

Created with Photoshop, a printer, and lots of card sleeves.

Dingga Ice Cream Shop

A quick project to promote one of my favorite K-pop group's music video. The music video showed the group members getting ice cream and so I came up with this simple mini game where the user puts together ice cream bars according to the orders displayed on the right. The mini game comes with achievements and hidden orders that can only be discovered by watching the music video and guessing what those secret orders are.

Created in Unity. Available via Unity WebGL (Desktop mainly).

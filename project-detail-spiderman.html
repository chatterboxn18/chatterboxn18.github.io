Spider-Man: No Way Home

My first project where I spearheaded the development side of the project. This is Trigger's final project with Sony for their Spider-Man Marvel trilogy. This application gives the users a glimpse of Peter Parker's phone and check out his new suits via AR. In this project, I led the multimedia infrastructure, the building the base UI, and worked closely with Sony's legal team to establish Google Firebase for analytics.

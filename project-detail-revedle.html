Revedle

When Wordle took my friends and the internet by storm, I wanted to join the fun by making my own Wordle with it's own theme and style. The simplicity of the game is brilliant and I wanted to see how long it would have taken for me to recreate it in Unity. After two weekends, I had this ready to go and it originally pulled from a Json of words relating to the K-pop group, Red Velvet. But when I ran out of words, I changed it to a scroll menu to choose different words to guess.

Created in Unity. Available via Unity WebGL (Desktop mainly).
